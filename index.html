HERE’S A BIT MORE ABOUT THE EVENT

CANDID
CHRONICLES

Moments that happen
naturally, memories that stay forever.

At Shiv's Photography, we believe the most beautiful moments are the ones that happen on their own — a quiet smile, a happy tear, a glance filled with love. Our candid photography style gently captures these real emotions as your day unfolds. Without interrupting the flow, we preserve your story just the way it is — natural, emotional, and timeless.

TRADITIONAL
PHOTOGRAPHY

Honouring rituals. Where
culture meets class.

Your weddings are full of meaning and tradition. From Thirumaangalyam to Mela Thalam, every step has a story behind it. We capture each ceremony with care — focusing on the details, the family bonds, and the beauty of your culture. With Shiv's Photography, your traditions are respectfully and beautifully preserved, creating memories to cherish for generations.

CINEMATIC WEDDING
STORIES

Your love, shot and told
like a movie.

What if your wedding film felt like a heartfelt movie — starring you? At Shiv's, our cinematic films gently weave together emotions, traditions, and joyful moments into a beautiful story that feels personal and real. From soft music to serene visuals, slow-motion thaali moments, laughter, and tears — it all flows like a timeless love story. More than just a video, it's a beautiful legacy, just like your love story.

In a world full of staged moments, we seek the ones that happen when no one's watching. A glance before the vows. A hand held tightly in silence. These are the frames that hold emotion at its purest. Whether it's a quiet backyard wedding or a place full of tradition, we focus on intimacy on scale. Because what matters most isn't how big the moment is, but how deeply it's felt.

Weddings are woven by many hands, and we believe in moving with the rhythm of every team around us. From planners to makeup artists, from decorators to surging crowds, we align, adapt, and collaborate with care. With our couples too, we don't just take direction, we listen, share, and co-create. Because when everyone flows together, the story unfolds effortlessly. And the magic feels real.

We don't interfere. We observe. Our style is soft, artful, and deeply honest. We'll guide you when you need us, but we'll also know when to fade into the background and let real moments unfold. There's no pressure to perform, no overdone posing. Just real emotion, real connection, styled with grace and captured with calm presence.

From the first conversation to the final delivery, we become part of your story. We learn your energy, your comfort zones, your people. By the time we're framing your moments, we already feel like we belong. We adjust your dupatta, laugh with your cousins, tear up with your parents. This connection lets us see more, feel more, and capture what truly matters — with heart, not just with skill.

Every photo we deliver carries not just beauty, but intention. We don't believe in fast edits or rushed stories. From the tones we shape to the final print you hold, quality is at the heart of everything. These aren’t just files — they’re future memories, meant to be revisited, held, framed, and passed down. Crafted slowly. Preserved beautifully. Timeless in every way.

Immerse yourself in the soulful world of visual poetry, where every frame is woven with feeling, every detail shaped with intention, and every story told with grace.

Founded by Shivani — an architect by education and a visual artist by heart — our studio is the result of years of passion poured into the art of storytelling through the lens. What began as a creative calling has grown into a dedicated team that thrives on connection, craft, and capturing love in its most honest, breathtaking form.

We believe that wedding photography isn't just about taking beautiful pictures — it's about understanding people, listening to their stories, and gently blending into their celebrations to preserve the moments that matter. From intimate home weddings to grand destination celebrations, our team works with a seamless blend of warmth and professionalism, building genuine relationships that reflect in every shot we take.

Our strength lies in the bonds we form — not just with couples, but with families, planners, artists, and fellow creatives involved in each wedding. We believe in collaboration over coordination, artistry over formulas, and meaning over trend. Whether it's a quiet ritual moment lit by soft temple light or an exuberant banquet in golden dusk, we move with purpose and presence, ready to catch the magic just as it unfolds.

Because at the heart of our photography is not just an image—but a feeling. One that lasts a lifetime.

Hi! I am Shivani,
the Photographer and Founder of
Shiv's Photography

I capture many kinds of stories, but weddings and baby photography are what I love most — those once-in-a-lifetime moments that deserve to be remembered in the most beautiful, timeless way.

An architect by education and an artist at heart, I have a natural eye for details — the way light paints a scene, the elegance in small gestures, and the emotions that make each moment unique. This is why my tagline, “Curating Beautiful Memories,” is not just a line — it’s my philosophy and that's how I approach every shoot.

My work blends artistry with luxury, for people who value photographs that feel personal, elegant, and everlasting — not just pictures, but heirlooms.

For me, photography has no boundaries. It’s not about chasing short-term trends or doing pushy marketing — I believe art finds its way to those who value it. If you’re here, maybe you’re one of them.

Every wedding I book is handled personally by me. I don’t delegate your special day to another team, because I want you to have my style, my focus, and my complete attention. Whether in my hometown or across the world, I’m always ready to travel to capture your story with care and artistry.

Why should we choose you? +−

Because if you’re looking for soulful, aesthetic photography and cinematic wedding films, you’ve already found the right place.

Do you cover destination weddings? +−

Absolutely. Travel fuels our creativity, every new place inspires us, and we’re eager to craft your destination into a timeless love story.

How is your work different from others? +−

We never look at others. Our only vision is to give our very best to the couples who trust us.

What makes Shiv’s Photography unique? +−

Because you chose us. Your story is our art, and we make it timeless.

Can we meet you before booking? +−

Of course. We love connecting with couples before their wedding. Whether in person or virtually, it’s a chance to understand your story and vision so we can create something truly personal.

How far in advance should we book Shiv’s Photography for our wedding? +−

We recommend booking at least 4–6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons. This ensures we can dedicate our full attention to your celebration and craft a timeless story around your big day.

Do you travel for weddings outside Nagercoil? +−

Yes, we’re available for destination weddings across India and beyond. Travel and stay are arranged based on the wedding location.

How do we confirm our booking? +−

Booking is simple. Once we understand the needs of your wedding events, your date will be secured with a confirmation amount (advance). This ensures our team is exclusively dedicated to your celebration.

Do you offer customizable wedding photography packages? +−

Absolutely, purely for your requirements. We offer signature collections, but every wedding is personalized to your vision and events.

Why should we invest in premium wedding photography? +−

Your wedding photos are more than just pictures, they are heirlooms. With Shiv’s Photography, you’re investing in artistry, storytelling, and emotion that will last for generations.

How many photographers will cover our wedding? +−

It depends on the scale of your events. For intimate celebrations, one or two photographers are enough. For grand weddings, we bring a carefully selected team to capture every moment without missing the details.

Will you guide us during portraits, or is it all candid? +−

We believe in blending both. Candid moments show your true emotions, while guided portraits add elegance and style. We’ll gently guide you when needed, so you always look natural and confident.

What is Shiv’s Photography style? +−

We blend fine-art elegance with natural storytelling. Our focus is on authentic emotions, luxurious tones, and cinematic compositions that feel timeless yet modern.

Will you cover both the bride and groom's preparations? +−

Yes. Our team ensures both stories are beautifully documented, the nervous laughter, the joyful tears, and the little details that complete your wedding narrative.

How long will it take to receive our wedding photos and films? +−

Sneak peeks are usually shared within the first 2 weeks. The complete gallery and films are delivered within 8–12 weeks, carefully edited to perfection.

Do you provide both photos and wedding films? +−

Yes, we offer complete photo + cinema coverage. From timeless stills to cinematic wedding films, your memories are crafted as a beautiful collection.

How will we receive our photos and videos? +−

Your memories are delivered in a premium online gallery (easy to share with loved ones) and in handcrafted albums or quality USB drives.

Do you edit all the photos? +−

Yes, every delivered image is professionally edited to reflect our signature Shiv’s Photography look – elegant, vibrant, and true to your wedding day.

Can the advance be adjusted if our wedding dates change? +−

We understand life can bring changes. If your dates shift, we’ll do our best to accommodate, depending on our availability.

Do you offer payment flexibility? +−

Yes, payments are usually split into easy parts — an advance to secure your date, a part on the event date, and the balance before delivery.
