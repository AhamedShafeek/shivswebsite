How to get Instagram embed URL:
1. Go to the Instagram post/reel on Instagram
2. Click the three dots (···) on the post
3. Click "Embed"
4. Copy the URL from the embed code (it looks like: https://www.instagram.com/p/ABC123/embed/)
5. Or just paste the regular Instagram post URL and we'll format it
